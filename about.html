About me

Zake is the pioneer of India’s graffiti scene and a legend among his contemporaries. Owing to his training in commercial arts, Zake has been imprinting his art onto Mumbai’s busy streets and commercial spaces for close to a decade now.

From spray painting in the city’s local gullys to representing India at the world’s biggest Graffiti Festival in Brazil, Zake has truly made a mark as India’s biggest graffiti art export. He believes that painting on the streets lets him talk to people from all walks of life. Always moving forward and inventing new ways to express himself, he blends European and urban styles, often adopting Indian scripts and elements.
