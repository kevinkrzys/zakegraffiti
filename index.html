Mumbai graffiti pioneer Zake, is on a mission to splash some color on this city. And he likes to be annonymous about it.

Zakes's graffiti is a road mix of many art styles that create this unique graffiti style. From spraying pertinent question marks on potholes on roads from Churgate to Virar stunning pieces of art that add edgy charater to the city, Zake and his brothers of the DIS crew are keeping it legit and real.

Graffiti artists are usually inconsistent and imitative, but Zake is highly promising and has benefited from the media exposure street art gets in Mumbai.

Zake is one the first generation Graffiti artists in India. His name resonates with everyone in the scene.
